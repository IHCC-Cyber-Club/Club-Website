About the IHCC Cybersecurity Club

A student-led organization focused on building skills, community, and real-world cybersecurity experience through collaboration and service.

Our Story

The IHCC Cybersecurity Club was formed in 2022 by students in the Cyber Security and Systems Administration program. Their goal was to build strong professional relationships while gaining hands-on experience that translates directly to the workforce.

Our journey is driven by innovation, customer-centricity, and simplifying complex technologies. Today, we continue pushing the boundaries of modern cloud infrastructure.
